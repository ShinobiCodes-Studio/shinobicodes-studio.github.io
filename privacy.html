Privacy
Policy

Updated February 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Hi, I am ShinobiCodes, the indie developer behind this studio. I am dedicated to building privacy-focused applications that respect your data and your attention.

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications developed by me, including Momo, AntiTODO, and future releases (collectively, the "Services").

By downloading or using my Services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. How I Handle Your Data

I prioritize your privacy above all else. My data collection practices depend entirely on which Application you are currently using.

A. Offline-First Apps (e.g., Momo)

These applications are designed to be 100% Offline.

  • Data Storage: All sensitive data (e.g., financial transactions, budgets, receipts, journal entries) is stored locally on your device.
  • No Cloud Sync: I do not have servers that process this data. I cannot see, access, recover, or sell your personal content.
  • Data Loss: Because your data is not synced to a cloud server, if you uninstall the app or lose your device without a local backup, your data is permanently lost. I cannot restore it for you.

B. Cloud-Enabled Apps (Future Releases)

For any of my future applications that may require internet access (e.g., for syncing tasks across devices):

  • Account Data: I may collect your Email Address for authentication purposes only.
  • Storage: User content for these specific apps will be stored securely using industry-standard providers (e.g., Google Firebase).

3. Permissions I May Request

I only request permissions that are strictly necessary for the app's core functionality.

  • Camera: (Used in Momo) To scan receipts or QR codes. Images are processed locally and never uploaded to my servers.
  • Biometrics (Fingerprint/FaceID): To lock/unlock the app. I do not store your biometric data; I only receive a "Pass/Fail" verification signal from the Android System.
  • Notifications: To send local reminders (e.g., bill reminders or daily check-ins).

4. Third-Party Services

I use a limited set of third-party tools to improve app stability. I do not use these tools to track your financial details or personal inputs.

  • Google Firebase Crashlytics: I use this to collect anonymous crash reports. This helps me fix bugs and improve stability. (Data collected: Device model, OS version, crash logs).
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: I track generic usage events (e.g., "Button Clicked," "Screen Viewed") to understand which features are popular.

Note: You can opt-out of analytics tracking in the "Settings" menu of my apps.

5. Advertising

I do not show advertisements in my applications. I do not use advertising IDs or sell your data to ad networks.

6. Children’s Privacy

My Services are not intended for children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If I discover that a child under 13 has provided me with personal information, I will immediately delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update my Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
