About
Us

Quiet Software / Indie Studio

Code in Silence. Data in Shadows.

We live in a noisy world. Every app wants your data, your attention, and your subscription.

SHINOBICODEs is my resistance.

I am an indie software studio based in India, dedicated to building "Quiet Software"—tools that do their job perfectly, then get out of your way.

My Philosophy

  • 1. Privacy is not a setting. It's the default. Like a Shinobi, my apps operate in the shadows. I build Offline-First. Your financial data, your journals, and your habits belong on your device, not on my servers. I cannot sell what I do not have.
  • 2. No Ads. No Noise. I do not clutter your screen with banners, pop-ups, or "rate me" nagging. I believe software should be calm, beautiful, and respectful of your focus.
  • 3. Built by Human Hands. There is no boardroom here. SHINOBICODEs is a one-person operation. Every line of code, every pixel, and every feature is crafted by me, with obsessive attention to detail.
